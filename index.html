Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28.02.2024

СОС Детско Село / Кибернасилство

is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that is not collected by our app, "Кибернасилство," and how we ensure your data's security.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our app, "Кибернасилство," is designed to provide educational content on cyberbullying without compromising your privacy. We do not collect any personal data, including but not limited to names, addresses, email addresses, or phone numbers. The app does not use cookies, trackers, or any form of analytics tools that gather personal information.

No Backend Data Collection

"Кибернасилство" does not connect to any backend servers. All content, including texts and images, are static and pre-loaded within the app. There is no mechanism within the app to collect, store, or transmit personal information of its users.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no information to share with third parties. Our commitment to your privacy means that your use of the app remains private and secure.

Children's Privacy

Given that our app does not collect personal information, it is safe and suitable for individuals of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
